About Our School

A legacy of excellence, built on values that shape tomorrow's leaders

📜 Our History

Established in 1870 as the Angulugaha Methodist Mixed School with just 12 students, Angulugaha Maha Vidyalaya has grown into a leading educational institution in the Galle District. Initially managed under the supervision of the Methodist Church, the school experienced rapid development after being taken over by the government in 1940. It was classified as a junior school in 1963 and was upgraded to a Maha Vidyalaya on September 1, 1977, adopting the name Angulugaha Maha Vidyalaya. Since then, through the development of infrastructure, educational resources, and various improvement projects, the school has continued to progress and today stands as a distinguished institution committed to providing quality education and nurturing excellence among its students.

Dear Students, Parents and Well-Wishers,

It is with great pride and deep humility that I welcome you to Angulugaha Maha Vidyalaya — a school that has stood for seven decades as a beacon of learning and character in our community.

Our school is more than just a place of academic instruction. It is a community where young minds are nurtured, where curiosity is celebrated, and where every student is given the opportunity to discover their unique potential.

We believe that excellence is not merely about examination results — though we pursue those with great dedication — but about developing young people who are compassionate, resilient, and ready to contribute meaningfully to our nation and the world.

I invite all stakeholders to work together with us in this sacred mission of education. Together, we will continue to uphold the proud traditions of our school while boldly embracing the opportunities of the future.

🎭 Our Student Clubs

Angulugaha Maha Vidyalaya hosts over 10 active student clubs spanning arts, science, sports, media and community service. Each club is available to support school events when booked in advance.
